## Refund Policy for Cancelled Competitions

If The Lucky Garage cancels a competition for any reason, the following refund policy will apply to ensure fairness, transparency and customer protection:

### 1. Automatic Refunds

If a competition is cancelled before the draw takes place, all entrants will receive a full refund of the ticket purchase price.

Refunds will be processed automatically using the same payment method used at checkout.

### 2. Processing Time

Refunds will typically be processed within 5 - 10 business days, depending on the entrantâ€™s bank or payment provider.

Entrants will receive a confirmation email or in-app notification once the refund has been initiated.

### 3. Promotional or Discounted Tickets

Any discounted, early-bird, or promotional tickets purchased for the cancelled competition will also be refunded in full, regardless of the discount applied.

### 4. No Substitute Prizes

In the event of cancellation, no substitute prize, cash alternative or credit will be offered.

Entrants will only receive the full monetary refund.

### 5. Wallet Balance (If Applicable)

If The Lucky Garage introduces or uses an in-app wallet system and a refund is required:
  - Refunds for wallet-funded purchases will be returned to the wallet.
  - Users may request a cash withdrawal of those refunded wallet funds if desired.

### 6. Partial Cancellations or Technical Failures

If a competition is invalidated due to a technical error, system malfunction or insufficient entries:
  - The entire competition will be cancelled.
  - All entrants will receive a full refund as per this policy.

### 7. Communication of Cancellation

All entrants will be notified of a cancellation via:
  - Email
  - SMS (where applicable)
  - In-app notification

Notifications will include confirmation of the cancellation and details of the refund process.

### 8. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following situations:
- If the competition has successfully run and the draw has taken place.
- If an entrant voluntarily withdraws or requests a cancellation of their own entry after purchase (unless required by law).

### 9. Compliance

This policy complies with the Consumer Protection Act and any additional South African regulations governing competitions, digital commerce and refunds.
