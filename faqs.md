# FAQs


# Essential Information

**If your question isn’t answered below, please contact us at (support@theluckygarage.co.za)**


### Is this a scam?

No.
The Lucky Garage is a legitimate skill-based competition operated by The Lucky Garage (Pty) Ltd, a South African registered company (Reg. No. 2025/876149/07).

All winners are announced within the app.


### Can I purchase tickets if I do not reside in South Africa?

Yes.
Entrants living outside South Africa may enter as long as it is legal in their country and they are able to lawfully receive or collect the prize within South Africa.

Before entering, please review clauses 9 - 12 of the **COMPETITION TERMS & CONDITIONS**.


### Where can I find my tickets?

1.   Tap the **Profile** icon at the bottom of the app.

2.  Select **Your Entries** on the next screen.

3.  If your tickets don’t appear, email us at (support@theluckygarage.co.za) and we’ll send you a screenshot of your Ticket Number(s).


### What if I forgot my password?

Please email us at support@theluckygarage.co.za for assistance.


### What should I do if I have a payment issue?

If you experience a payment problem, simply contact us at (support@theluckygarage.co.za) and we’ll assist you


### How do I enter a competition?

1.   Tap the **Competitions** icon at the bottom of the app.

2.  Choose the competition you’d like to enter and tap **Enter Now**.

3.  Answer the question and use the slider to select how many tickets you’d like. Only correct answers are included in the draw.

4.  Tap **Add to Basket**.

5.  Tap **Pay ZAR** to complete your purchase.

### Who can enter?

Anyone aged 18 or older may enter the competitions.


### How is the winner chosen?

Winners are selected at random from all correct entries during a live draw using a verified random number generator.

If you win, please ensure we have your correct contact information - otherwise another winner may be selected.

We also love celebrating winners, so we may feature your prize moment on our social media!
